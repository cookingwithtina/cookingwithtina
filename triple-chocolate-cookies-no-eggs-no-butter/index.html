Triple Chocolate Cookies (no eggs and no butter)

by TinaPosted onApril 25, 2022June 5, 2022

Triple Chocolate Cookies are yummy, soft and light treats that are very quick to prepare and due to the presence of chocolate are reminiscent of the flavor, texture and appearance of brownies. They are made with an eggless and butterless shortbread with a soft and tender texture to the bite, these cookies are great for all occasions: for breakfast or snack for the little ones, for a sweet moment during the day or for tea time.

  • 200 gr of flour ’00
  • 30 gr of bitter cocoa
  • 60 ml seed oil
  • 100 gr milk chocolate
  • 100 ml hot water
  • 80 gr of brown sugar or classic sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 80 gr of dark chocolate chips
  • salt to taste

If you also want a dairy-free version, just use dark chocolate instead of milk chocolate (I recycled some Easter egg!

Mix the flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder and a pinch of salt in a bowl. Add the chocolate chips and mix well. Separately, melt the milk chocolate and mix with the oil, then heat the water a few minutes and pour everything into the chocolate bowl. Then pour the flours into the chocolate, stir with a whisk and wait for the mixture to cool and set at room temperature; you should get a moist but not sticky mixture. Scoop out balls of the mixture with your hands or with the ice cream scoop tool and place them on 2 baking sheets previously lined with parchment paper. Bake the double chocolate cookies in a preheated static oven at 180° for 10 minutes. Then let them cool to room temperature before enjoying.
