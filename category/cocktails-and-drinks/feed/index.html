]]> Pineapple, yogurt and ginger smoothie is a thirst-quenching, refreshing, digestive and nutritious drink made with pineapple, white yogurt, fresh ginger and turmeric. This diet shake, rich in vitamins and minerals, is perfect any time of day, for breakfast, as a snack or to end a meal.

 

Pineapple is an exotic draining and digestive fruit, a source of organic acids (malic acid, citric acid) and trace elements (calcium, phosphorus, magnesium, potassium; rich in vitamin C and beta-carotene; contains bromelain, an enzyme found in the stem that has an anti-inflammatory action. Ginger is a spice that has an important antioxidant action and protects the skin from free radicals. Turmeric is a spice known for its antioxidant, immune-stimulating, anti-arthritic, digestive and choleretic properties.

 

Serves 2 people

 

  • 380 g fresh pineapple pieces
  • 1 piece of fresh ginger root
  • 125 g white yogurt
  • a pinch of turmeric powder
  • juice of 1/2 lemon

 

Clean the pineapple by removing the rind and the stringy center, then cut it into pieces and collect them in the glass of a blender.

 

 

Peel the ginger root, cut it into pieces and place it inside the blender. Add the yogurt, turmeric and lemon juice; blend at high speed until you get a creamy mixture; pour into two glasses and serve immediately.
